About

SIGNLY is a web based application.This project goal is to take the simple step in connecting the social and communication bridge between regular people and the disabled people with the help of Sign Language.As our project only deals with alphabets, it can be extended to common expressions and also words which can be more effective for disabled and normal people in communication and understanding. As we live in a century where world developing at a rapid pace in terms of digital and technological advances,this project could be one of the stepping stones where technology meets humanity and help the hearing impaired and mute community.
